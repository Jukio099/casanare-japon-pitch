Casanare: Epicenter of the Energy Transition

Casanare is Colombia's energy capital (63% of gas, 16% of oil). This base funds a robust ecosystem of renewable energies, including biomass and large-scale solar projects.

Decisive Competitive Advantage:

Casanare has the cheapest energy in Colombia, a factor that drastically reduces operating costs for any industry.
